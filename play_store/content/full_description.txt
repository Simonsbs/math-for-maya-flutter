Math For Maya helps children build confidence with vertical arithmetic in a calm, encouraging format.

Designed for focused practice, the app presents one equation at a time and keeps all controls on a single touch-friendly screen.

What children can practice:
- Addition
- Subtraction
- Mixed mode (random addition/subtraction each question)

Learning-focused features:
- Vertical equation layout (school-style)
- Carry and borrow helper buttons
- Hint button for guided support
- Check answer and show solution actions
- Positive, gentle mistake feedback to encourage retrying
- Confetti celebration on correct answers

Flexible setup:
- Choose 1 to 4 digits
- Choose fixed rounds (5, 10, 15) or Endless mode
- Fast restart with setup-first flow

Built for kids:
- Large touch controls
- Clear visual hierarchy
- No ads
- No in-app purchases
- No account required

Math For Maya is made for repeated daily practice and confidence-building through positive reinforcement.
